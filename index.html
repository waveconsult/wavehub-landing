Good to know.

01Is WaveHub a bookmaker?+

No. WaveHub is an analysis and consulting service. No money is staked, held or moved through us. We are on your side of the line, not the house's.

02How does the application work?+

You apply, we review every application personally and come back by email. If it fits, you get access and create your account. Quality of the group beats quantity.

03How do you get a better price than most bettors?+

We are early. We find a price that is higher than the matchup deserves, before the market corrects and the line moves. Taking that value, again and again, is the edge.

04How many picks per day?+

Few and high-conviction, not daily noise. Some days there is nothing worth betting, and we will say so. The bets that do come are tightly structured.

05Can I use it on desktop and phone?+

Yes. One account everywhere: desktop, mobile and the installable app. Log in once and you are in across all your devices.

06How does payment work?+

Once your application is accepted, we send you a secure Stripe payment link by email. Memberships are yearly. No money moves inside the app itself.
